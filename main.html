Welcome to our NeighbourHood Link's ride-sharing platform!

With our platform, drivers can easily share their ride details, including the start and end locations, available seats, vehicle type, and any special features such as wheelchair accessibility or pet-friendliness, as well as allowing you to suggest a petrol contribution method.

Riders can share similar information and have the flexibility to decide how much they want to contribute towards the ride.

Once a ride is agreed upon, both parties become "established neighbors." Drivers can rest assured that they won't receive additional ride requests once all seats are filled, providing a hassle-free experience for everyone involved.

Join our community today and make your commuting experience more enjoyable and eco-friendly!
