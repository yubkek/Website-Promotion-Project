FAQ

Question: Can I book the ride for me and multiple friends?

Answer: Yes, riders can specify the amount of people they have with them for the driver to see.

Question: What if I don't want to pay for the driver's petrol?

Answer: You can negotiate with driver on a set price.

Question: Is an account necessary to use the service?

Answer: Yes, you will need a registered account to safely put in your details.

Question: How does billing and payment work?

Answer: You will be given a range of different online payment options to choose from, and will be charged at the end of the ride.
